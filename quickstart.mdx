Welcome to Gigue. This guide will walk you through the initial setup to get your multiplayer AI workspace fully operational.

## 1. Sign In
Navigate to [demo.gigue.app](https://demo.gigue.app/) and click **"Sign In With Google"**. Follow the prompts to create your account.

## 2. Connect Your Core Tools
Gigue needs access to your communication and data tools to provide intelligent insights. Navigate to the **Connections** tab to link your accounts:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Slack" icon="slack" color="#4A154B">
    Toggle Slack and follow the authorization flow to sync your channels and messages.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Gmail" icon="envelope" color="#EA4335">
    Connect your Gmail. *Note: If you see a verification warning, click **Advanced** and then **"Go to Gigue (unsafe)"**.*
  </Card>
  <Card title="Google Calendar" icon="calendar" color="#4285F4">
    Sync your schedule to allow AI agents to prepare meeting briefings.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Google Drive" icon="google-drive" color="#34A853">
    Enable Drive access so AI agents can reference your documents for deal context.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## 3. Sync & Ingest (Closed Alpha)
If you are part of our closed alpha, you may need to manually trigger the initial data sync in the **Back Office** panel:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sync Channels">
    Navigate to the **Back Office** section, select **Gmail** as the source, and click **Run**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ingest Messages">
    Select **Gmail** under **Ingest Messages** and click **Run** to process the raw data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize AI Context">
    Use **Group Conversations** and **Generate Workstreams** to let Gigue's AI begin organizing your accounts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 4. Explore Your Workspace
Once your data is synced, use the left sidebar and top navigation to explore:

- **Home (Daily Briefing)**: Your central hub. See AI-generated briefings on your day, upcoming meetings, and extracted action items.
- **Conversations**: View your multi-channel messages (Slack/Gmail) grouped by account or topic.
- **Workstreams**: Track specific deals or projects. Gigue automatically extracts tasks and updates from your conversations.
- **Unified Search (Cmd + K)**: Instantly find any message, document, or contact across all connected platforms.
